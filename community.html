Where to Dance in Victoria

Swing City

Run by Denny's Canned Music for over 13 years, Swing City is a social dance including all styles of ballroom! Held on most Friday nights at the Edelweiss German Cultural Center in James Bay, there is always a free one hour dance workshop to kick-off the evening. The dance is on Fridays with the doors opening at 7:45pm and a beginner lesson from 8:00 pm to 9:00 pm. Social dancing is from 9:00 pm to 12:00 am.

Price: $10 per person.

*Due to COVID restrictions, there is currently no social dancing available. Email dancevictoria1@shaw.ca for the most up to date information

Salsa Caliente

Salsa Caliente is currently located at 727 Yates St, Victoria. Salsa classes are taught Mondays and Tuesdays, while bachata and merengue are on Thursdays. Social dances may become available in September, so check back here for updates.

Price: $12 per class, $5 for a social dance.

Red Hot Swing

Tentatively beginning shortly after September 7th, 2021, Red Hot Swing will be offering drop-in intro lessons and social dancing to DJed music every Monday night (with a live band performing once every month), as well as a drop-in intro lesson followed by social dancing to live music every Friday. No partner or experience is required, but we reocommend you pack a spare sense of humour! Whether you're mad about lindy hop, charleston, balboa, or collegiate shag, we will have you dancing the night away in no time.

Our Monday night dances are held at Norway House (1110 Hillside Ave) and our Friday night dances will be at Dance Victoria (2750 Quadra St)

NOTE: To attend the dances, you will be required to show proof of vaccination for COVID-19 each time you enter (we will be doing this until the end of October and then continuing as necessary)

Price (with band): $14 adults, $13 members, $11 for students
Price (with DJ): $9 adults, $8 members, $6 for students

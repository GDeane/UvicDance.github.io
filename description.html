Intermediate Salsa on 1

LA style salsa places strong emphasis on sensuouseness, theatricality, aerobics and musicality. We will start with a "shines" portion of the class to practice footwork and technique so that you know what to do when your partner lets go of you on the dance floor. This class will continue to introduce you to the essentials of partnerwork by starting with the basic steps and moving though to advanced partner combinations.

Beginner Salsa on 2

NY style salsa (mambo) is danced to the rhythm of the congas and the techniques used allows for quick turns and direction changes, complex partner patterns and more body movement while dancing on a crowded dance floor. This class will introduce you to the essentials by starting with the basic steps and moving though to intermediate partner combinations. Learn to move your body to the rhythm of Latin music and practice leading and following techniques to get you ready to move to the next level.

Student Salsa Team

Join our brand new performance team! The team will consist of dedicated dancers who will learn a challenging routine with the option to perform at some local/campus events. Improve your technique, spinning, body movement, stage presence and more while practicing with dancers that want the same thing as you do- to improve while having fun! Never performed or even danced before? No problem! No partner or experience is required. You just have to be dedicated and have a positive attitude. All levels will be challenged to improve.

Intermediate Latin Ballroom

Continue to build on the skills learned in the beginner class and add new moves to your repertoire!

Beginner Standard Ballroom

In Standard Ballroom discover the romance of the Waltz, the smoothness of the Foxtrot, the excitement of the Quickstep and the passion of the Tango. Ballroom dancing is still a popular and mesmerizing social activity that is an exhilarating experience for the mind, the body and the soul and does wonders for your love life. Come out and learn the basic elements that will get you started on a life-long journey of romance and magic.

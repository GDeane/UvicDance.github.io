One Minute Interviews

Want to be featured on our official Instagram account and share your story? We are starting a new mini series this term where we will feature a different member of our club each week; you'll get a chance to talk about your history (or maybe lack thereof) with dance, your relationship with the club, and any other dance related tidbits from your life! We are happy to hear fun and lighthearted stories, or personal and intimate anecdotes-- whatever you feel is the most you, we want to hear it.

If you would like to participate in this series, please fill out this application form and we will reach out to you about interview times.
